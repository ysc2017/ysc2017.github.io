YSC 2017 Video competition

You would like to tell us about your research; however, cannot participate in person at Gold Coast? Worry not! Just send us a video!

The Young Statisticians Conference 2017 presents YSC 2017 Video Competition for those who are unable to attend the conference. We would love to hear about your research. In addition, you might win some exciting prizes!

Key dates:

  • Submission deadline: 15 August 2017
  • Result Announcement: 27 September 2017

Fee

None!

Rules and regulations:

  • You must be a student or an early career researcher (within 5 years of graduation) of statistics or a related field
  • One participant can submit only one video
  • Video length is limited to 1-3 minutes. Any video over 3 minutes will be automatically disqualified
  • Video must not have any slides, props or additional media (music, subtitles etc.)
  • The language of the video must be English; no songs or poems are allowed

Video submission process

  • Record and upload the video in Youtube with the privacy setting as ‘Unlisted’
  • Put your name in the ‘Title’ section and “YSC 2017 Video Competition” in the ‘Description’ section of the video
  • Register for the competition and put your details (using the form below)
  • Copy your video URL from Youtube and paste it in the designated space in the registration form

Will be announced soon.
